Founded by Hasnat Ali

Founder Hasnat Ali in his trimester starts learning Web development. Falls in love with development and starts doing projects for his business colleagues. Within the same year starts offering services on Fiverr.com.

Level One on Fiverr

After finding love and passion for Development founder Hasnat Ali starts to offer small services on Fiverr and Achieves Level one on Fiverr.

First LLC is registered

After finding success in selling smalls services founder Hasnat Ali decides to make this more than a hobby and founds an LLC and names it 4amDevelopemt.

First Core team

The first core team is assembled in Pakistan. The company starts doing mainly web development work for local firms and slowly established its local presence. Company Achieves level 2 on Fiverr.

Infiniti.dev is Born

More employees join the company in Islamabad as 4amdevelopment establishes itself locally. The company, extremely grateful to its loyal clients and their wonderful reception, grows more and more within the Pakistan market and it’s reborn as Infiniti.dev.
